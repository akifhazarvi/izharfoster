Muhammad Musa

Graduate Management Trainee — Izhar Foster.

Role

Refrigeration, panels, and the electrical side of cold storage.

Muhammad Musa is a Graduate Management Trainee at Izhar Foster — the cold-chain division of Izhar Engineering. His day-to-day work involves inspection of chiller units, PIR sandwich panels, and maintenance of refrigeration systems (semi-hermetic compressors) for HVAC building applications.

He holds a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering from COMSATS University Islamabad (2021–2025). Before joining Izhar Foster, he worked as an Electrical Designer at Bilal Switchgear Engineering on AutoCAD Electrical and panel wiring projects, then took on freelance EPLAN Electric P8 and control panel design work via Upwork. His final-year work covered relay coordination using ETAP, grid-tied PV system design at 33/11 kV, and UAV-based power line inspection.

He writes on the Izhar Foster blog about cold storage market dynamics, controlled-atmosphere stores, prefabricated structures, insulated doors, and Pakistan's growing agri-export cold-chain infrastructure.
