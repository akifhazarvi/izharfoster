How "near me" actually works

Why a single Lahore plant beats a local fabricator down the road.

Engineering, not guesswork

A local fabricator typically sizes a cold room by rule of thumb. We run a full ASHRAE Refrigeration Handbook Ch. 24 heat-load calculation for every project — transmission, infiltration, product, internal, and equipment loads — tuned to your city's design ambient and humidity. The result is a cold store that holds temperature in a heatwave and doesn't waste power the rest of the year. You can preview the numbers yourself in our free load calculator and cost calculator.

Factory-grade panels

Our FireSafe PIR panels come off a continuous production line in Lahore — consistent thickness, consistent λ 0.022 W/m·K insulation value (BS EN 14509), B1 fire class, food-grade facings. Locally hand-laid panels vary joint to joint and degrade faster. The transport cost of bringing real panels to your city is small next to the energy and reliability cost of the alternative.

One company, full warranty

Panels, refrigeration plant, insulated doors, and installation all come from Izhar Foster. There's no finger-pointing between a panel supplier, a refrigeration contractor, and a builder when something needs attention — it's one accountable company, in business since 1959, with a service network that reaches the whole country.
