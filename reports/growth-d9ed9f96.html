12 — What to do next

Four moves the data points to.

Every recommendation below comes straight from a number in this report — ranked by return for the effort involved.

01

Own the pricing-query cluster

"…price in pakistan" terms (sandwich panel, PUF sheet, PUF panel, roof, 4×8) are the fastest-rising theme and already sit top-5. Build a dedicated pricing/cost hub and wire in the Cost Calculator — this is exactly where new demand is landing.

02

Recover two queries that slipped

"cold storage business in pakistan" fell to pos 28 (was 5.4) and "cold store manufacturers in pakistan" to pos 22 (was 4.7) — classic signs of a URL that moved in the redesign. Check redirects and re-point internal links; low effort, high return.

03

Harvest striking-distance impressions

"sandwich panel" (514 impressions, pos 5.9, just 1.8% CTR) and the pharmaceutical cold-storage page (1,073 impressions, pos 11.2) have the visibility but weak click-through. Rewrite titles and meta descriptions to convert traffic already being served.

04

Build city landing pages

Lahore already ranks (18 clicks, pos 7.5). Karachi (289 sessions), Islamabad (158), Faisalabad (66) and Multan (47) show clear demand with no dedicated page. Clone the Lahore template for each — a fast, repeatable win in Pakistan's biggest markets.

05

Lean into AI citations

~41 visits already come from ChatGPT, Copilot & Perplexity, landing on the technical pages. Keep the llms.txt current and publish more spec-rich, citable content (named case studies, engineering guides) — this channel compounds while competitors ignore it.

06

Clean the analytics signal

Apply the Vercel-Singapore bot filter and set up the AI-Assistant channel group in GA4, so the analytics number tells the same honest story the search data does before it reaches stakeholders.
