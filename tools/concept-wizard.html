How this wizard works behind the scenes

Five questions, one structured WhatsApp message. No bot pretending to be a human. No SaaS chat fee. Pakistan B2B sales runs on WhatsApp — we lean into that rather than fighting it.

  1. Your answers stay in your browser as you progress — if you reload, you don't lose your progress.
  2. On submit, the five answers are formatted into a structured message and a WhatsApp window opens prefilled at our sales-engineering line (+92 321 5383544).
  3. You tap Send in WhatsApp — that's the actual handoff. We see the structured brief immediately.
  4. Within 24 working hours, an engineer responds — not a chatbot, not a salesperson reading a script. The actual person who will design your cold store.

If you'd rather email, the long-form quote page sends the same structured brief via email. Both routes land at the same desk.
