PIR panels — 49× more insulating than concrete. Made in Pakistan.

We manufacture ozone-friendly Polyisocyanurate (PIR) sandwich panels with thermal conductivity as low as 0.028 W/m·K, density 60 kg/m³, fire class B1, and a 50+ year service life — produced in our own factory on Multan Road, Lahore.

PIR sandwich panels are the building envelope behind virtually every modern cold storage facility, food factory, warehouse, and energy-efficient industrial building. Izhar Foster manufactures ozone-friendly PIR panels — described as "a revolutionary development in Pakistan" — and discontinued legacy polyurethane (PU) production years ago in favour of safer, more sustainable technology.

Thermal performance you can quantify

Insulating effect is approximately 49 times greater than concrete and 42 times greater than brick at equivalent thickness. Thermal conductivity is as low as 0.028 W/m·K. The result is roughly 30% savings on energy and maintenance over the building's life.

Environmental and safety

  • Eco-friendly pentane blowing agent — zero ODP, no CFCs, no HCFCs
  • Fire-resistant with flame-retardant properties
  • Fire category B1 (foam B2) per ASTM E84 testing
  • Non-hygroscopic — completely waterproof and moisture-proof

Construction advantages

  • Lightweight yet structurally strong
  • Panels available up to 50 feet long
  • Core thickness up to 250 mm
  • Self-assembly tongue-and-groove design reduces construction time and cost
  • Lifespan exceeding 50 years

Frequently asked

PIR Sandwich Panels — questions buyers ask.

The technical and commercial questions we hear most often before a contract is signed.

01What thicknesses do you make?
Standard cores are 50, 80, 100, 125, and 150 mm. Specialised applications up to 250 mm. Cold rooms below −20°C usually use 100–150 mm.
02How does PIR compare to PUR?
PIR has lower thermal conductivity and significantly stronger fire resistance. We discontinued PUR after WHO concerns over chemicals and gases involved.
03Are panels delivered ready to install?
Yes. Tongue-and-groove edges allow rapid self-assembly with our installation teams or qualified subcontractors.
04What's the panel lifespan?
Over 50 years with stable thermal performance. PIR is dimensionally stable (<1% drift) and non-hygroscopic.
