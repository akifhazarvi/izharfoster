The Group

Founded by Engineer Izhar Ahmad Qureshi in 1959 — when Pakistan was twelve years old.

The late Engineer Izhar Ahmad Qureshi earned a highly reputed name in the veteran engineer's community of Pakistan. Since its inception in 1959, the Group has now entered its 6th decade of pioneering construction operations — graduated into a multifaceted organisation with authority over various innovative and economical building techniques.

The introduction of pre-fabricated steel buildings and mechanical fabrication revolutionised industrial construction in Pakistan and cemented the Group's supremacy as the largest multidimensional engineering entity in the country. The recently established Izhar Housing brings affordable luxury living, while the parent activity of pre-casting continues to produce a wide variety of high-quality structural concrete members.

Izhar Foster — the cold-chain division — applies the same engineering rigour to the temperature spectrum: from −40°C industrial blast freezers to controlled-atmosphere fruit stores at +1°C. Every facility we build keeps something alive: a fruit grower's harvest, a vaccine on its way to a clinic, a dairy that supplies a city.
