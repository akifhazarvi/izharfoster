The short answer

If you are choosing a sandwich panel core in Pakistan, the decision comes down to three materials: EPS, PUF/PU, and PIR. They look identical on the outside — the same steel facers, the same cam-lock joints — but they perform very differently, and their prices reflect that.

Price by thickness

Core material sets the baseline; thickness moves the invoice. PIR runs the highest, PUF ~0.85× and EPS ~0.60× at the same thickness. Price your exact spec in the Panel Price Calculator.
