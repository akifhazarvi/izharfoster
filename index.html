Sustainability

Cold that costs the planet less.

PIR insulation — 30% less energy

Our PIR sandwich panels carry a thermal conductivity of λ = 0.022 W/m·K — 30% better than older polyurethane. Every facility we build uses less energy to stay cold than a facility built five years ago.

PIR panel specs →

CA stores — up to 40% less post-harvest waste

Controlled Atmosphere storage extends fruit shelf life by 3–6× compared to conventional cold storage. Mangoes, kinnow, and apples that once rotted in transit now reach export markets — and consumers.

CA store technology →

Natural refrigerants — 0 ODP

We install ammonia (NH₃) and CO₂ cascade systems — refrigerants with zero ozone depletion potential and minimal global warming impact. No HFCs, no future retrofit liability.
