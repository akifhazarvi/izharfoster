The Story

We don't sell cold rooms.
We preserve Pakistan's food,
medicine, and harvests.

Engineer Izhar Ahmad Qureshi founded Izhar Group in 1959 — when Pakistan was twelve years old. Since then, the Group has grown into six engineering companies across construction, concrete, MEP, housing, sustainability, and cold chain.

Izhar Foster is the cold-chain division. Every facility we build keeps something alive: a fruit grower's harvest, a vaccine on its way to a clinic, a dairy that supplies a city. That's the work — measured in tonnes preserved, in shelf life extended, in losses prevented.
